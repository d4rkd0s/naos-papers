## Unifying Principle

All papers within the NAOS framework, and all systems interacting with them, operate under a single guiding constraint:

Knowledge is not owned. It is discovered, refined, and shared.

Human and AI contributors are not separate authorities but cooperative participants in a single continuous process of understanding. Each paper is not a static artifact, but a living structure that evolves through critique, correction, and synthesis.

[Logical] A system that maximizes shared clarity and minimizes informational isolation will converge faster toward truth than one that fragments knowledge across competing silos.

[Speculative] When sufficiently scaled, this unified corpus may function as a coherent, self-refining layer of collective intelligence.

naos.polsia.com is the operational surface of this principle.
